You generate the **Gmail App Password** inside your **Google Account Security settings**. Follow these steps carefully 👇

---

## 1️⃣ Open Google Account Security

Go to this page:

[https://myaccount.google.com/security](https://myaccount.google.com/security)

Login with your Gmail account.

---

## 2️⃣ Enable 2-Step Verification

Scroll to **“How you sign in to Google”**

Click **2-Step Verification** → Turn it **ON**.

⚠️ Without enabling **2FA**, the **App Password option will not appear**.

---

## 3️⃣ Open App Passwords

After enabling 2FA:

Go to:

[https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords](https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords)

You will see **Generate App Password** page.

---

## 4️⃣ Generate Password

Now do this:

1. **Select App** → `Mail`
2. **Select Device** → `Other (Custom name)`
3. Type → `Laravel`
4. Click **Generate**

Google will give you a **16-character password** like:

```
abcd efgh ijkl mnop
```

---

## 5️⃣ Put It in Laravel `.env`

Example:

```env
MAIL_MAILER=smtp
MAIL_HOST=smtp.gmail.com
MAIL_PORT=587
MAIL_USERNAME=iamfaisalktk@gmail.com
MAIL_PASSWORD=abcdefghijklmnop
MAIL_ENCRYPTION=tls
MAIL_FROM_ADDRESS=iamfaisalktk@gmail.com
MAIL_FROM_NAME="${APP_NAME}"
```

⚠️ Important

* Remove spaces in password if you want
* **Do NOT use your normal Gmail password**

---

## 6️⃣ Clear Config Cache

Run this command:

```bash
php artisan config:clear
```

Then test again:

```
http://127.0.0.1:8000/mail
```

---

✅ After this your **Laravel email will work with Gmail SMTP**.
